🦊 About the studio

Small creative studio. Big stack of coloring pages.

Bliss Fox Studio creates approachable, giftable coloring books for adults and kids. We focus on charming themes, clean printable formats, and collections that work for both casual colorists and dedicated coloring book fans.

What we make

Coloring books and printable pages built around cozy, giftable themes. Each collection is designed to be relaxing, accessible, and fun — whether you're a first-time colorist or a longtime enthusiast.

  • Cute character-driven themes (animals, holidays, cozy scenes)
  • Clean, printable page layouts for home printing
  • Physical paperback editions via Amazon KDP
  • Digital printable downloads via Etsy

Why Bliss Fox

Bliss Fox Studio started as a small side project focused on one goal: make coloring books that people actually want to keep, gift, and share. We believe great coloring books should be approachable — no complicated pages, no intimidating themes, just cozy illustrations that help you unwind.

Every book goes through the same process: pick a theme, sketch clean line art, test print on standard paper, and refine until the pages feel satisfying to color.
