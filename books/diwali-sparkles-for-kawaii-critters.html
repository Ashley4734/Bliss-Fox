About this book.

Diwali Sparkles for Kawaii Critters: A Cute Coloring Book for Adults & Kids turns the Festival of Lights into the coziest coloring session of the season. Every page is packed with rounded, chibi-style animals decorating homes, sharing sweets, and glowing with warm Diwali cheer.

It is built to be approachable. Bold, clean outlines make it easy for younger colorists to fill in, while the charming background details give older fans plenty to slow down and enjoy.

What is inside.

Decorative home scenes

Sweet kawaii critters decorating homes, stringing lights, and arranging festive treats.

Festival treats & symbols

Lamps, flowers, and Diwali sweets drawn in soft, approachable line art.

Calm, joyful moments

Warm themes without anything intense. Just cozy, feel-good coloring.
