About this book.

Spooky Sweet Halloween Cutie Pals is the comfort version of Halloween. Every page leans into the fun parts of the season: little animals in costumes, grinning jack-o-lanterns, friendly ghosts, and piles of treats, all in a soft, huggable kawaii style.

It makes a perfect October ritual or seasonal gift. Big, clean shapes are easy and relaxing to color, while the playful details keep it fun page after page.

What is inside.

Friendly frights only

Smiling ghosts, cheerful bats, and costumed cuties. Spooky vibes, zero nightmares.

Costumes and candy

Trick-or-treat scenes, pumpkins, and sweets with bold, satisfying outlines.

Seasonal gifting

A cozy October treat for kids and adults, and a ready-made Halloween gift.

Who it is for: Great for Halloween fans who like their spooky on the sweet side, families looking for a seasonal activity, and anyone collecting cozy autumn coloring books.
