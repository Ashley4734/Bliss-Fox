About this book.

Cozy Coffee Shop Critters is a slice-of-life coloring book set in the warmest little cafe in town. Adorable animals sip drinks, share pastries, and lounge among houseplants and string lights, all rendered in a soft, comforting kawaii style.

It is made for unwinding. The scenes are gentle and inviting, with enough cozy detail to keep your pencils happy without ever feeling overwhelming.

What is inside.

Cafe critters

Cats, hedgehogs, and friends enjoying lattes and treats in a snug coffee shop.

Comfort details

Steaming mugs, pastries, houseplants, and warm interiors to fill with color.

Pure relaxation

Calm, low-stress pages designed for slow evenings and creative breaks.

Who it is for: For coffee lovers, cat people, and anyone who wants a soft, slice-of-life coloring book that feels like a quiet morning at your favorite cafe.
