Privacy Policy

How Bliss Fox Studio handles the limited information collected through this website.

Effective date: June 4, 2026

Plain-English summary: Bliss Fox Studio uses this website as a small brand hub for coloring books and printable downloads. We do not sell personal information, we do not process payments on this site, and purchases happen through Etsy or Amazon.

This Privacy Policy explains how Bliss Fox Studio ("Bliss Fox Studio," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you visit blissfoxstudio.com and related pages on this website. It applies only to this website. It does not apply to purchases, accounts, messages, shipping, or payment activity handled by Etsy, Amazon, Pinterest, Google, or any other third-party platform.

Information this website collects

We keep data collection on this website limited. Depending on how the site is configured at the time you visit, the following information may be involved:

  • Information you choose to send: if you contact us through a marketplace profile, we may receive your name, email address, message, and any details you choose to include.
  • Basic technical information: web servers, security tools, and hosting providers may automatically process information such as IP address, browser type, device type, referring page, pages visited, and the date/time of the visit.
  • Marketplace information: when you click to Etsy or Amazon, those platforms may collect purchase, payment, shipping, account, and browsing information under their own policies.

This website does not directly collect credit card numbers, payment details, shipping addresses, marketplace account passwords, or sensitive personal information.

How we use information

  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the website.
  • To respond to questions, support requests, or customer messages you send.
  • To understand which pages, books, or links are useful so we can improve the site and product lineup.
  • To comply with legal, tax, security, fraud-prevention, or platform requirements when applicable.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use this website to collect personal information from children.

Cookies, analytics, and embedded services

This site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site functionality, security, analytics, performance, or affiliate link tracking. You can control or block cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work the same after blocking them.

The site may load resources from third parties, including Google Fonts, Amazon image/CDN resources, Etsy, Amazon, Pinterest, and hosting or security providers. Those third parties may receive technical information such as your IP address, browser details, and the page where the resource was loaded.

Affiliate and marketplace links

Some links on this site point to Amazon, Etsy, or other marketplaces. As an Amazon Associate, Bliss Fox Studio may earn from qualifying purchases. Affiliate links do not change the price you pay.

When you click a marketplace or affiliate link, you leave this website. Your activity on Etsy, Amazon, or any other marketplace is controlled by that platform's terms, privacy policy, cookies, payment processing, shipping rules, and customer service systems.

Third-party platforms

Bliss Fox Studio uses third-party platforms to sell and promote products. These may include:

  • Etsy for digital downloads, listings, shop messages, and customer support.
  • Amazon for paperback books, orders, payments, fulfillment, and customer service.
  • Pinterest or other social platforms for profile pages, pins, promotion, and referral traffic.

We do not control those platforms' privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies directly before using them.

Children's privacy

Bliss Fox Studio creates coloring books and printable pages that may be used by families, children, teens, and adults. However, this website is intended for adults, including parents, guardians, teachers, gift buyers, and shoppers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and we do not ask children to submit personal information through this site. If you believe a child provided personal information to us, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as reasonably needed for the purpose it was provided, such as responding to a message, handling records required by a marketplace, or meeting legal and security obligations. Marketplace order records are retained by Etsy, Amazon, or other applicable platforms according to their own policies.

Your choices and privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you. You may also have the right to opt out of certain uses, where applicable. Because purchase data is handled by Etsy or Amazon, requests about marketplace orders, payments, shipping, or account data should be directed to the relevant platform.

For information we control directly, contact us using the method below and describe your request clearly.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards appropriate for a small website and brand studio. No website, email system, or online platform can guarantee perfect security, so please avoid sending sensitive personal information through ordinary messages or forms.

International visitors

Bliss Fox Studio is based in the United States. If you visit the site from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or by third-party providers in other countries where privacy laws may differ from those in your location.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of the page shows the latest revision. Material changes will be reflected by updating this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact Bliss Fox Studio through the Bliss Fox Studio Etsy shop. If a dedicated privacy email address is added later, this page will be updated with that address.
