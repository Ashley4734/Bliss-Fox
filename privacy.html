Privacy Policy

How Bliss Fox Studio handles information on this brand website and when visitors click through to Etsy or Amazon.

Back to home

Last updated: June 4, 2026. This page is a practical website privacy policy for Bliss Fox Studio. It is not legal advice.

Overview

Bliss Fox Studio creates coloring books, printable coloring pages, and digital downloads. This website is a simple brand hub that links visitors to the official Etsy shop for digital products and to Amazon for physical paperback coloring books.

Information we collect

This website does not currently process orders, accounts, checkout, or payments directly. Any purchases are completed through third-party marketplaces such as Etsy or Amazon.

The email signup form on the homepage is currently a placeholder and is not connected to an email marketing service. If it is connected in the future, this policy should be updated to describe what is collected and how subscribers can unsubscribe.

Information collected automatically

The website may generate ordinary server logs through its web host or deployment platform. Those logs may include information such as IP address, browser type, pages requested, referring URLs, and timestamps. This information is used for basic security, troubleshooting, and site performance.

Third-party marketplaces

When you click a link to Etsy or Amazon, you leave this website and interact with that marketplace directly. Their privacy policies, cookies, order handling, payment processing, shipping, refunds, and account systems apply.

  • Etsy shop: https://www.etsy.com/shop/BlissFoxStudio
  • Amazon author page: https://www.amazon.com/Bliss-Fox-Studio/e/B0GZFBTS87/

Cookies and analytics

This website does not currently include a dedicated analytics platform or advertising pixel. Third-party services, embedded marketplace images, browser fonts, hosting providers, Etsy, and Amazon may use their own cookies or request logs according to their own policies.

Children’s privacy

Bliss Fox Studio products may include family-friendly coloring themes, but this website is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through a connected service in the future, contact the relevant marketplace or service provider.

Data sharing

Because this website does not currently operate its own checkout or connected email signup, Bliss Fox Studio does not sell personal information collected through this site. Third-party marketplaces and infrastructure providers handle their own data under their own policies.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the site adds analytics, email marketing, contact forms, advertising pixels, direct checkout, or other features. The “Last updated” date will be revised when meaningful changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions about a purchase, account, refund, or download, please contact Etsy or Amazon through the marketplace where the purchase was made. For Bliss Fox Studio brand questions, use the official Etsy shop contact tools.
